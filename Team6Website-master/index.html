DEVELOPMENT BLOG

13/10/19 - Video trailer announcing Area 51

Hey guys, Team 6 is proud to announce a new game idea and it’s called AREA 51. We chose AREA 51 since its an interesting and interactive topic that people in our age demographic would relate to. With our timelines flooding with the Raid Area 51 memes, we figured that creating a Game based around it, would create additional hype, and we can’t wait to get to work. Stay tune for more.


16/10/19 - Team meeting

Hey guys, we came up with an amazing storyline and created conceptial sketches of our characters that would possibly be featured in our game. Also we gathers ideas of the challanges that awaits, the further the player progresses through the levels in the game.


23/10/19 - Team meeting 2

Our team agreed with the concept of adding up to five levels, with each level increasing the chances of randomly encountering an enemy. Our current game strategy objective is for the player to escape the heavily guarded facilities while dodging enemies that stumble upon their journey throughout the game.


30/10/19 - Team meeting 3

Hey guys, we finally finished our storyline and decided that the enemies would be defined as the army, which consists of army personnel and tanks (with an occasional monster thrown in the mix). You the character is trapped in AREA 51 and need to break out of the facilty to regain your freedom


10/11/19 - Team meeting 4(The Hack)

Our hack for the game is completed with the basic functionality of the game, however it contains alot of bugs and the game is far from complete


10/11/19 - Website

Hey guys our website is up and running. The website still needs a lot of work so, stick around for more updates.


18/11/19 - Team meeting 5

Quick update we fixed major bugs in the game and we are now progressing in creating further level in the game.


30/11/19 - Team meeting 5

The game is in its final stages of completion for the MVP(minmal viable product) and it would be available for downlaod during the MVP presentation 12/12/19


30/11/19 - Website Completed

Here's quick update, the foundation of our website is now completed, hopefully all information of the game is available to you on our website. Also, regular maintainance will be carried out during midnight.


5/12/19 - Team meeting 6

Our MVP is finally ready for the presentation MVP event taking place at 12/12/19, be sure to watch our presentation and any question you may have would be answered during this event


12/12/19 - Presentation of MVP (minimal viable product)

The wait is finally over and we are excited to announce our game, also our MVP would be available for download on the day of presentation. Be sure to tune in for more awesome updates we make to the game!
